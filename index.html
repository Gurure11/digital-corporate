About Prime Queens Beauty Salon



--- Welcome to Prime Beauty Queens, your premier destination for exquisite hair and nail services in Kempton Park! With years of dedicated
service in the industry, we have established ourselves as a beacon of excellence in hair treatment, styling, and nail care.



At Prime Beauty Queens, we understand the importance of looking and feeling your best. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of services
tailored to meet your unique beauty needs. Whether you're in search of a stunning hair makeover or a relaxing nail treatment, our team of skilled
professionals is here to exceed your expectations.



Our expertise in hair care encompasses a diverse array of styles, including straight up, sweet sour, free hand, kiddies free hand, bonding,
razor-cut dreads, wool styles, and two lines. Additionally, we specialize in gents' haircuts, offering precision cuts such as chiskp, brush cut,
trim, and taper, among others. Whatever your preferred style, our talented stylists are committed to delivering results that leave you looking and

feeling fabulous.


In addition to our styling services, we also offer a range of hair treatments, including hair dye, relaxer, and intensive treatments to keep your
locks healthy and vibrant. Plus, our braiding services are perfect for those looking to add flair and sophistication to their hair.



At Prime Beauty Queens, client satisfaction is our top priority. We pride ourselves on providing a welcoming and inclusive environment where you can relax
and indulge in a moment of self-care. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to some well-deserved pampering, we're here to
make your beauty dreams a reality.
